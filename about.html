  • From its roots as the oldest bank in Binangonan, Rizal, with a mission to extend credit to small farmers and merchants, BRB has now evolved into the most progressive and innovative Rural Bank in the whole of the Philippines.

  • BRB is the 1st Rural Bank in the Philippines to launch E-Money issuance operations.

History

BRB was started by Atty. Teofile Tanjuatco and authorized by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas to operate as a rural bank

Quezon Capital Rural Bank together with the families of Herbert Bautista and Renato Mercado acquired the majority stake in the Company

An Australian company Ausphil Technology Pty. Ltd. acquired majority of the company with the intent to digitally transform the bank.

The Company increased its authorized capital from PHP 11M to PHP 25M

The Securities Exchange Commission approved the extension of BRB's corporate life for another 50 years

The Company increased its authorized capital from PHP 11M to PHP 25M

BRB was granted a conditional Electronic Money Issuer (EMI) license by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP)

Applied for a further P100M increase in authorized capital, subject to BSP and SEC approval, bringing total authorized capital to P200M.
