Balance Inquiry

Since BRB is a BancNet registered digital bank, you can check balance at
any ATM or POS terminal with BancNet logo.


Withdrawal

In the Philippines, 98% of all transactions are still done by cash even though
Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas has been aiming and encouraging all Filipinos
to increase cashless transaction. There will be times that cashless is not
available from where you shop or dine. Using your card, you can
withdraw your needed cash at any BancNet ATM and POS terminal.


Bills Payment

 Securely pay your bills at any ATM/POS terminal near you.
 Local billers from telecommunications to credit cards to utility is available.
 Real time posting.

Purchase

We love shopping online because we hate the long lines and the volume of people at a store. BRB cards can be used on your online and offline transactions. Grocery, dinning, gas, shopping, one swipe and you are paid! Even if you are on a store, it will not take you several minutes just to wait for change. No more annoying coins, no more insufficient change; just sakto.

Payroll

Managing a business is never easy especially since this involves money
from restocking to your employee's salary. Open a account with us
and easily distribute your employee's salary directly to their cards.

1. Open an account.
2. Funds must be available at least a day before payday.
3. Send us the Debit/Credit note.
4. Money will be transferred instantly with minimal fees.

E - Banking

By this time, cashless is being encouraged though most people prefer cash because it is what they are used to. Most of the time we are doubtful when trying to be cashless. With e-Banking, you can do all bank transactions at your fingertips. You can securely access your account because it will be protected with one-time-passwords (OTP) that will be sent to your registered mobile number.

Download the app, create a profile and you are all set.
Access it on web with the same log on information as your mobile banking. OTP will be sent to your mobile number.

E - Load

Need load? Reload on the nearest ATM or POS terminal.

Remittance

Safely and securely send money from all around the world directly to your family's cash card. No need for your family to fill out forms, no more long lines, no more wasted time and effort because an SMS notification will be sent to their registered mobile number as soon as the amount is available on the cash card.


Fund Transfer

All cardholders can transfer money either from BRB to BRB Card, or BRB to other banks’ card. This feature can be done from any BancNet ATMs and POS terminals. This can also be considered as local remittance.


Mobile Banking

Make the best out of your mobile phone with banking on-the-go! No need to go to a physical store for your banking needs. Create profile, log on, and you're all set.

Online Banking

You only need an internet connection and everything will be done in a jiffy. No hassle.

E-wallet

Securely store your money on this digital wallet that you can carry anywhere. Purchasing process made easy and simple for your convenience. Start paying your goods and services the digital way!
