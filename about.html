  • From its roots as the oldest bank in Binangonan, Rizal, with a mission to extend credit to small farmers and merchants, BRB has now evolved into the most progressive and innovative Rural Bank in the whole of the Philippines.

  • BRB is the 1st Rural Bank in the Philippines to launch E-Money issuance operations.
