Balance Inquiry

Since BRB is a BancNet registered digital bank, you can check balance at any ATM or POS terminal with BancNet logo.


Withdrawal

In the Philippines, 98% of all transactions are still done by cash even though Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas has been aiming and encouraging all Filipinos to increase cashless transaction. There will be times that cashless is not available from where you shop or dine. Using your card, you can withdraw your needed cash at any BancNet ATM and POS terminal.


Bills Payment

 Securely pay your bills at any ATM/POS terminal near you.
 Local billers from telecommunications to credit cards to utility is available.
 Real time posting.

Purchase

We love shopping online because we hate the long lines and the volume of people at a store. BRB cards can be used on your online and offline transactions. Grocery, dinning, gas, shopping, one swipe and you are paid! Even if you are on a store, it will not take you several minutes just to wait for change. No more annoying coins, no more insufficient change; just sakto.

E-Banking

By this time, cashless is being encouraged though most people prefer cash because it is what they are used to. Most of the time we are doubtful when trying to be cashless. With e-Banking, you can do all bank transactions at your fingertips. You can securely access your account because it will be protected with one-time-passwords (OTP) that will be sent to your registered mobile number.


E-Load

Need load? Reload on the nearest ATM or POS terminal.

Remittance

Send money from all around the directly to your family’s cash card. It is safe and secure. No more forms to fill out, no more lines just to tell you that your transaction is not in their system or it is still for clearing. No more wasted time and effort because an SMS notification will be sent to their registered mobile number as soon as the amount is ready for use.


Fund Transfer

All cardholders can transfer money either from BRB to BRB Card, or BRB to other banks’ card. This feature can be done from any BancNet ATMs and POS terminals. This can also be considered as local remittance.


Mobile Banking

Banking on the gowhere you can make the best of all your banking needs. No need to you to a physical bank or ATM. Just log on your phone and you're all set.

Online Banking

Do you banking needs but can't leave the office or your home? We have online banking! You only need the internet connection and everything will be done in a jiffy

E-wallet

A digital wallet stores your money safely and securely. Paying for your purchased goods and services has never been this easy. No annoying forms to fill out before confirming each payment. Simplified process is for the customer convenience. It is like paying cold cash in a digital way.
