Balance Inquiry

BRB is a member of BancNet. You can check your balance at any ATM and POS terminal with the BancNet logo.


Withdrawal

In the Philippines, 98% of all transactions are still done by cash even though Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas has been aiming and encouraging all Filipinos to increase cashless transaction. There will be times that cashless is not available from where you shop or dine. Using your card, you can withdraw your needed cash at any BancNet ATM and POS terminal.

Bills Payment

Securely pay your bills at any ATM/POS terminal near you. Local billers from telecommunications to credit cards to utility is available. Real time posting.

Purchase

We love shopping online because we hate the long lines and the volume of people at a store. BRB cards can be used on your online and offline transactions. Grocery, dinning, gas, shopping, one swipe and you are paid! Even if you are on a store, it will not take you several minutes just to wait for change. No more annoying coins, no more insufficient change; just sakto.

Payroll

Managing a business is never easy especially since this involves money from restocking to your employees' salary. Open an account with us and easily distribute your employees' salaries.

1. Open an account.
2. Funds must be available at least a day before payday.
3. Send us the Debit/Credit note.

E-Banking

Most of the time we are doubtful when trying to be cashless. With e-Banking, you can do all bank transactions at your fingertips. You can securely access your account because it will be protected with one-time-passwords (OTP) that will be sent to your registered mobile number.


Download the app, create a profile and you are all set.

Access it on web with the same log in information as your mobile banking. OTP will be sent to your mobile number.


E-Loading

Need load? Reload on the nearest ATM or POS terminal.

Remittance

Safely and securely send money from all around the world directly to your family's cash card. No need for your family to fill out forms, no more long lines, no more wasted time and effort because an SMS notification will be sent to their registered mobile number as soon as the amount is available on the cash card.
